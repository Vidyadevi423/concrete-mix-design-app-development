How to Use the App

1

Sign In or Register

Create a free account or log in to access the full system and save your designs securely.

2

Open Mix Design

Click "New Mix Design" from the sidebar. Select concrete grade, aggregate size, and exposure details.

3

Enter Parameters

Fill in slump value, W/C ratio, admixture %, and standard deviation as per your project requirements.

4

Calculate

Hit the Calculate button. The system follows IS 10262:2009 to compute all material quantities.

5

Review Results

View cement, water, fine & coarse aggregate masses, mix ratio, and estimated cost per m³.

6

Save Design

Save the design with a name. Access it anytime from Saved Designs. All data is stored in the database.
