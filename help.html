Help & Support Centre

Quick guide, FAQs, and standards notes for the Nominal Mix (IS 456) calculator.

How to Use the Calculator

1

Open “New Mix Design”

Go to the Mix page and choose the concrete grade (nominal mix ratios M5–M20).

2

Set Quantity

Enter Length/Breadth/Thickness to auto-calculate volume, or uncheck auto mode and type volume directly.

3

Enter Mix Inputs

Adjust dry volume factor, W/C ratio, cement bag weight, and densities (optional) for your local materials.

4

Optional: IS 456 Durability Check

Select Concrete Type + Exposure to see max W/C, min cement content, and min grade warnings (IS 456 Table 5).

5

Optional: Cost Estimate

Enter cement/sand/aggregate/water prices to get Total Cost and Cost per m³ for budgeting.

6

Save & Export

Sign in to save designs to your account. Use Export Report to generate a printable summary.

Standards Note

📘
Nominal Mix (Site Estimation)
This tool uses nominal volumetric ratios (M5–M20) and adds an optional durability check based on IS 456:2000 (Table 5). For higher grades and formal design mixes, use IS 10262 mix design procedures and lab trials.

Support

Common Issues

If results look wrong, double-check units (m, m³, liters), and ensure prices are entered in ₹ per bag / ₹ per m³.

Browser Storage

Reports are prepared using local storage. If export is blank, re-calculate once and try Export Report again.

Contact

For help with login or saved designs, contact your administrator or site support team.
